About Us

Your Sound, Perfected

Founded in 2010, Vibo Records has established itself as a premier destination for artists, producers, and content creators seeking top-tier audio production. Our team of experienced engineers and producers are passionate about helping you realize your Vibo vision.

We believe that great sound starts with a great environment. Our acoustically treated spaces and carefully curated equipment selection ensure that every recording, mix, and master meets the highest standards of quality.

Whether you're an emerging artist or an established professional, we provide the tools, expertise, and Vibo collaboration to make your project shine.

Our Services

Recording

Professional recording sessions in our acoustically treated live room and isolation booths, with premium microphones and preamps.

Mixing

Expert mixing services to balance, enhance, and bring clarity to your tracks using analog outboard gear and premium plugins.

Mastering

Final polish and loudness optimization to ensure your music sounds its best on all playback systems and platforms.

Audio Post

Comprehensive audio post-production for film, TV, and video including dialogue editing, sound design, and mixing.

Production

Full music production services from songwriting assistance to complete track creation with our in-house producers.

Podcasting

Professional podcast recording, editing, and mixing with voice optimization and sound branding services.
